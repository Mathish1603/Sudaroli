About Us

Rooted in local taste and evening tradition.

Our History

From a humble stall to a favorite dinner stop.

Sudaroli Evening Parotta Stall began with a simple promise: serve hot, soft parottas at fair prices when the town slows down for dinner. Over the years, our stall has become a familiar place for people who want filling South Indian food made fresh in front of them.

Our menu stays focused because we believe a few dishes, made well every day, can build real trust.

Mission

To serve fresh, affordable, and delicious parottas every evening with warm hospitality and dependable quality.

Vision

To become the most trusted evening parotta destination in the neighborhood while keeping our food honest and homely.

Our Promise

Care in every plate.
